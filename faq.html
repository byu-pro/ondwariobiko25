Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We've got answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

What services do you offer?

I specialize in a range of services including graphic design (branding, logo design, marketing materials), web design (UI/UX), and front-end development (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React). I can take a project from the initial concept and branding all the way to a fully functional, coded website.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary greatly depending on the scope and complexity. A simple branding project might take 1-2 weeks, while a full website design and development could take 4-8 weeks or more. After our initial discovery call, I'll provide a detailed proposal with a projected timeline.

What is your design process?

My process is collaborative and structured. It typically includes:
1. Discovery: Understanding your goals and audience.
2. Strategy & Research: Market research and strategic planning.
3. Design: Creating wireframes, mockups, and prototypes.
4. Development: Bringing the designs to life with code.
5. Launch & Handover: Deploying the project and providing you with the necessary assets.

How much do your services cost?

As every project is unique, I provide custom quotes based on the specific requirements. I offer project-based pricing to ensure transparency with no hidden fees. Please reach out via the contact page for a detailed, no-obligation quote.

Do you offer website maintenance?

Yes, I offer monthly maintenance and support packages to keep your website secure, updated, and performing optimally. This can include software updates, security scans, content updates, and performance monitoring. We can discuss these options after your project is launched.
