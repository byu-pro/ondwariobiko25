About Me

My Journey as a Digital Craftsman

I'm Ondwari Obiko, a multi-disciplinary designer and developer dedicated to creating seamless and memorable digital experiences. My journey began in graphic design, where I learned the fundamentals of visual communication and brand identity. This foundation led me to web design, where I discovered the power of translating static visuals into dynamic, interactive interfaces.

Today, I specialize in front-end development, using my combined skills to build performant, user-centric websites and web applications. My work is a blend of artistic vision and technical precision, ensuring every project is not only beautiful but also built to last.
